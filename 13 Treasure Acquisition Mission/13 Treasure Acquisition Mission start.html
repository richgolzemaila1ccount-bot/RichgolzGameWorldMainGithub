Treasure Acquisition Mission

You are a spy.
You have been assigned a mission to acquire a treasure.

That treasure is hidden within the wall directly in front of you.
There is a slot where you can place a stone piece.

You must choose the correct stone piece
and place it into the slot
to acquire the treasure.

Will you Try?
