13 Treasure Acquisition Mission



Treasure Acquisition Mission

You are a spy, tasked with securing the treasure hidden within the wall before you.

You’ve successfully made it to the treasure’s location.
Now, you stand directly in front of the wall.

The treasure lies somewhere inside.
If you enter the correct answer, part of the wall will open, allowing you to claim it.

In front of you are stone fragments shaped like numbers.
The wall has slots where these number pieces can be placed.

Etched into the wall is what appears to be a code:

Triangle = 3
Square = ?
Pentagon = 5
Which number piece will you choose?
