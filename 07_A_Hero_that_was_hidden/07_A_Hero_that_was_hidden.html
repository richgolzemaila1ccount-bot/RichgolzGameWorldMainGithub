07 A Hero that was hidden



A Hero that was hidden

You are a hidden hero—
someone who has planned and is now carrying out a secret operation.

You created a button that, with a single press,
could influence random people around the world.

You were planning to press it.

You made before the button can be pressed,
a password must be entered.

But wait…
It seems the password on the control screen has been changed.

It looks like some clever spy has altered your password.

You’ll need to figure out what the new password is.

Enter the correct password and press the button.

*
This game requires a bit of note-taking.
Please prepare paper and a pen,
or use a notes app on your laptop or phone.
