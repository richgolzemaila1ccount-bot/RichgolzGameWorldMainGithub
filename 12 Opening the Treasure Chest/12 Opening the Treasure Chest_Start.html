Opening the Treasure Chest

You found a treasure key and a treasure chest in the Briarwood Maze.

You returned home
and decided to open the chest a few moments later.

When you come back to the table
where you left the chest and the key,
you notice something strange.

The single key has become three keys.

Someone—or some-being—
may have added more keys to interfere with you.

Will you try to find the correct key?
