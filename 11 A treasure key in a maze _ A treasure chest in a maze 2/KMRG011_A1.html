A treasure key in a maze

You found the right path.

Maybe it was a kind of joke—
leave, leaving, left…

It looked like it was leaving,
so the answer was “left.”

Haha.
Very funny.

You let out a small, dry smile,
directed at the creator of this maze—at least, in your mind.

In the center of the maze,
you find a room-like space.
Inside it, you discover a chest containing a key.

You take the chest
and make your way out of the maze.
