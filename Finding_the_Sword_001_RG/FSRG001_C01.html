Finding the Sword RG001



        You arrive at the riverbank.

        Holding the newly forged sword, you stand before the river.
        With a heart that feels both nervous and excited,
        you begin to lower the blade toward the surface of the water.

        The river is unimaginably deep.
        And impossibly wide.
        It is so vast that it could almost be called a sea.

        Is there anyone who could cross to the other side?
        You wonder as you gaze toward the distant, barely visible opposite shore.

        As you continue to wash the sword,
        you absentmindedly glance downward.

        The river is unfathomably deep.

        It feels as though you are staring into a profound abyss.
