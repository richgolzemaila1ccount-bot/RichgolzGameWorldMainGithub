16 Assigning Numbers to Direction



Assigning Numbers to Direction

The correct answer is 3.

Think of a clock.

The left statue points upward—
just like 12 o’clock.

The middle statue points left—
just like 9 o’clock.

The right statue points to the right,
which corresponds to 3 o’clock.

Therefore, the correct answer is 3.

You get your treasure from the opened statue,
you close it,
and make your way out.

You’ve successfully escaped
with the treasure in hand.

Congratulations!
