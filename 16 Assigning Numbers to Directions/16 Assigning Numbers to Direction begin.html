16 Assigning Numbers to Direction



Assigning Numbers to Direction

Three statues stand before you.

The leftmost piece points upward
and is written the number 12 in front of it.

The middle piece points to the left
(from your point of view)
and is written the number 9 in front of it.

The rightmost piece has no written number.
It points to the right.

What is the correct number?
